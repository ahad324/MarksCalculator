     Obtained Marks: 19
     Max Marks: 40
     Weightage: 10
     So, write the data accordingly in the fields and click the submit button.

4) Your Marks are also being updated in the Result Card.
5) If you want to update the marks for a subject, then you can copy that name from the Result Card and paste it in the Subject name field. Then you can add more assessment marks for that subject.
6) Grades are Given According to University Criteria.
7) You can also print the Result.
