Navigating Your Academic Journey: Understanding GPA, SGPA, and CGPA

What is GPA , SGPA and CGPA

GPA , SGPA , CGPA plays an important role in the student's acedemics.Now let's see what they are :

GPA

GPA stands for Grade Point Average, representing a student's performance in a specific course or subject.

SGPA

SGPA stands for Semester Grade Point Average, indicating a student's performance within a single semester.

CGPA

CGPA stands for Cumulative Grade Point Average, representing the overall performance of a student throughout their entire degree or program.

Understanding Grading Criteria

At the heart of our Gradegenie calculator lies a comprehensive grading criteria that assigns numerical values to your academic performance. Let's take a closer look at this criteria:
